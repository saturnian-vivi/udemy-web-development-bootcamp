The Best Movies According to Vivi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kung Fu Panda

My go-to rewatch movie for its comedy and characters. I especially love the fact that the main character is a heavy-weight panda who is the strongest as opposed to its real life nature.

Cars

All the characters and its message in subtle ways about the importance of friends and life lessons make it a fun yet comforting watch.

Hotel Transylvania

I love it for the take on its characters and how they all seem realistic in their comedic yet stereotypical personalities as humans as well as mythical creatures.
